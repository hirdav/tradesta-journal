The Arena Manifesto

"I enter this market not to gamble, but to hunt. The market is a machine designed to transfer money from the impatient to the patient. Today, I choose to be the patient."

1. MY AMMO IS SCARCE ($100)

I do not have infinite lives. I have two bullets ($50 each). If I fire blindly, I die. I will not spray and pray. I will wait for the perfect shot. Cash is a position.

2. MY WEAPON IS DANGEROUS (50x Leverage)

This leverage can build my empire or sever my hand. I respect it. I acknowledge that a 1.5% move against me is death. I will never enter a trade without knowing exactly where I will die (Stop Loss).

3. MY ENEMY IS THE FEE

The exchange wants me to churn. I will not feed the beast. I WILL NOT PAY FOR LIQUIDITY. I use Limit Orders only. If price leaves without me, let it go. Chasing is for losers.

4. MY TRIGGER IS DISCIPLINE

  • No FOMO: I don't care about green candles, only my plan.
  • No Revenge: If I lose, I walk away for 10 mins.
  • One Shot: 1-2 high quality trades > 10 mediocre ones.

5. THE PLEDGE

If I catch myself entering a Market Order... I will close the terminal.
If I catch myself moving a Stop Loss... I will close the terminal.
If I catch myself trading out of boredom... I will close the terminal.

LET THE GAMES BEGIN.
